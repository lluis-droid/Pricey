Quick Start
  1. Go to Bot Settings — set ticket category, log channel and staff role.
  2. Go to Payment Methods — add your accounts for each method.
  3. Go to Language — choose English or Spanish for the bot.
  4. Go to Purchase Panels — create your first panel and publish it.
  5. Users click the button in Discord to open a purchase ticket automatically.
