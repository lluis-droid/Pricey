Quick Start
  1. Go to Settings — ticket category, log channel, staff role, and bot language.
  2. Go to Payments — accounts for sales and, if you want, enable donations.
  3. Go to Panels — create your first panel, add coupons if you like, and post it.
  4. Users automatically open a ticket by clicking the Discord button.
  5. Check Tickets to approve/reject and view the blacklist.
