2. Description of the Service

Pricey is a third-party Discord bot and web dashboard that helps server owners manage ticket-based storefronts. Core features include:

  • Purchase panels — embeds posted to Discord channels that buyers click to open a private ticket.
  • Automated ticket channels — private channels where the bot walks buyers through questions, pricing, and payment.
  • Price calculation — configurable currency conversion and margin-based pricing for game currencies and digital goods.
  • Product and coupon management — fixed-price product lists and discount codes (percent or fixed amount, with usage limits and expiry).
  • Payment flow — the buyer selects from up to 23 supported payment methods, sees the seller's account details, and submits proof of payment (reference number and screenshot) for manual review.
  • Ticket transcripts — auto-generated when a ticket is closed, approved, rejected, or refunded.
  • Web dashboard — a browser-based panel for configuring the bot, managing tickets, viewing activity, and exporting data.

Pricey is an independent product not created, endorsed, or affiliated with Discord Inc.

4. Payments & Transactions

Pricey does not process, hold, transmit, or have access to any money. It has no integration with any payment gateway, bank, card network, or cryptocurrency exchange.

Pricey works as follows: the server owner configures their own payment instructions (a PayPal email, a crypto wallet, a Nequi number, and so on) in the dashboard. When a buyer opens a ticket, the bot displays those instructions and the calculated price. The buyer then pays directly to the seller through the external method, returns to the ticket, and submits proof of payment — typically a transaction reference and a screenshot. Staff review the proof and manually approve or reject the order.

All money movement happens entirely outside Pricey and outside Discord, directly between buyer and seller. Pricey does not verify that a payment was actually received. Any disputes about a purchase must be resolved between the buyer and the server operating the ticket. We strongly recommend that server owners publish clear refund and dispute-resolution policies before enabling paid features.

You are responsible for ensuring that anything sold through the Service complies with applicable law and with Discord's policies.

5. Ticket Data & Transcripts

When a buyer opens a ticket, the bot collects information needed to complete the transaction: answers to the server owner's custom questions (text, numbers, image uploads, or Discord user mentions), the selected product or currency amount, chosen payment method, transaction reference, and any screenshot uploaded as proof of payment.

When a ticket is closed, approved, rejected, or refunded, the bot generates a transcript of the conversation and stores it alongside the ticket record. Transcripts are used so that server staff can review past orders and resolve disputes. They are stored on the same infrastructure as all other ticket data, tied to the specific Discord server.

Server staff with access to the ticket channel — and the bot's owner for operational purposes — can view ticket contents. Buyers should be aware that anything they write or upload in a ticket channel may be read by staff and retained in the transcript.

6. Moderation

Pricey includes moderation tools that server owners and the bot operator can use:

  • Per-server blacklists — server admins can block specific users from opening tickets in their server.
  • Global bans and suspensions — the bot operator can temporarily or permanently block users across all servers that use Pricey. Banned or suspended users cannot open new tickets until the ban or suspension expires or is lifted.
  • Report flag — staff can mark a ticket user as "reported" for review by the server owner or the bot operator.

Moderation decisions made through the dashboard are final for the scope of Pricey. They do not affect a user's Discord account or their access to the server itself — only their ability to open tickets through the bot.
